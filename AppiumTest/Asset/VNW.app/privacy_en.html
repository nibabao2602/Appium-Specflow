What cookies are and how they are used

As part of offering and providing customizable and personalized services, the VietnamWorks Website career website may use cookies to store and sometimes track information about you. A cookie is a small amount of data that is sent to your browser from a web server and stored on your computer's hard drive. Several features of the VietnamWorks Website recruiting website require that you accept cookies in order to work properly. (See "What choices are available to you regarding collection, use, and distribution of your information" for more information on cookies.)

Generally, we use cookies for the following purposes:

(1) To identify and label all "new" jobs since your last visit to the site.

(2) Optionally to permanently save your username and password on your computer so you don't have to re-enter it each time you visit our site.

(3) To enable you to "checklist" jobs that you want to mark for future retrieval and viewing.

Advertising networks that serve jobs onto the VietnamWorks.com career website may also use their own cookies.

VietnamWorks will also gather or may gather certain information about your use of our site, such as what areas you visit and what services you access. Moreover, there is information about your computer hardware and software that is or may be collected by VietnamWorks. This information can include without limitation your IP address, browser type, domain names, access items and referring website addresses.
